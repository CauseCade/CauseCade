About Page

CauseCade is an educational tool created to explain Bayesian Networks to users. It does not seek to outperform existing Bayesian network tools. The difficulty of the course is undergraduate at worst, but we hope to make it accessible to high school students by making continuous improvements and thereby increasing accessibility.

Help

If you just got here and have no idea what to do, I recommend reading the blog or taking the tutorial. The blog can be accessed by the corresponding button on the top right of this page. The Tutorial can be accessed by going to: Learning Mode > course:tutorial > lesson:tutorial L1
